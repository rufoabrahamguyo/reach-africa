Strategic Objectives

Our roadmap for creating sustainable,
transformative change across Africa.

01

Strengthen Community Resilience

Enhance the ability of vulnerable communities to withstand and recover from social, economic, and environmental shocks.

02

Promote Child Development and Education

Improve access to quality early childhood development, education, and nurturing care for children and families.

03

Advance Sustainable Livelihoods

Support inclusive and climate-resilient economic opportunities that improve household income and food security.

04

Promote Health and Community Well-being

Strengthen integrated approaches that link human, animal, and environmental health for healthier communities.

05

Champion Sustainable Stewardship

Advance responsible management of natural resources to protect ecosystems and ensure long-term sustainability.

06

Strengthen Partnerships and Institutional Capacity

Build strong collaborations and internal systems to ensure accountability, effectiveness, and sustainable impact.

Program Pillars

Education and Child Development

Promoting access to quality education, early childhood development, and nurturing care to ensure children grow, learn, and thrive in safe and supportive environments.

We deliver early childhood development programs, school support and infrastructure, and child protection initiatives in partnership with communities and local authorities.

  • Early childhood development programs
  • School support and infrastructure
  • Child protection initiatives

Resilient Livelihoods and Economic Empowerment

Strengthening sustainable livelihoods through agriculture, entrepreneurship, skills development, and market access.

We support farmers and families with climate-resilient practices, vocational training, and linkages to markets.

  • Agricultural and pastoralist support
  • Entrepreneurship and skills training
  • Market access and value chains

Health and Well-being (One Health)

Advancing community health through integrated approaches linking human, animal, and environmental health.

One Health initiatives that improve nutrition, disease prevention, and access to care.

  • Community health outreach
  • Nutrition and WASH programs
  • Animal health and zoonosis awareness

Food Security and Sustainable Agriculture

Improving food systems and agricultural productivity for reliable access to safe, nutritious, and sufficient food.

We work with smallholder farmers and pastoralists to strengthen food production and storage.

  • Climate-smart agriculture
  • Post-harvest and storage
  • Diversified and nutritious diets

Environment and Creation Care

Promoting sustainable management of land, water, and natural resources to protect ecosystems and strengthen climate resilience.

Creation care and environmental stewardship are core to lasting community transformation.

  • Natural resource management
  • Climate adaptation and mitigation
  • Conservation and restoration

Community Leadership and Institutional Strengthening

Building the capacity of community institutions, local leaders, and organizations to drive inclusive and sustainable development.

We invest in local governance, leadership training, and organizational development.

  • Leadership and governance training
  • Community-based organization support
  • Partnership and coordination capacity
