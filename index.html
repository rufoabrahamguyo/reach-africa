OUR VISION

A transformed Africa where communities are resilient, children flourish, and families achieve sustainable well-being with dignity, inclusion, and opportunity.

OUR MISSION

To transform communities by advancing resilience, ending poverty and hunger, nurturing children and families, and promoting sustainable development through strategic partnerships.

Our Core Values

Faith and Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, accountability, and commitment to Christian principles in all we do.

Compassion and Human Dignity

We respect the inherent value of every person and serve communities with empathy and justice.

Resilience and Innovation

We embrace adaptive, evidence-based approaches that strengthen communities to withstand shocks and stresses.

Child and Family Focus

We prioritize the well-being, protection, education, and holistic development of children and families.

Sustainable Stewardship

We promote responsible care of natural resources, advancing creation care and environmental sustainability.

Partnership and Collaboration

We work collaboratively with communities, governments, private sector actors, faith institutions, and global partners to achieve lasting impact.
